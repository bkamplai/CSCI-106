Class Goals:

ASL 1 Period 1:

  • Extend our ASL vocabulary to use it to have conversations in class and with the Deaf community
  • Apply ASL to outside activities, hobbies, or jobs
  • Have confidence in our signing ability and know that mistakes help us grow

ASL 1 Period 2:

  • To learn about and understand Deaf culture
  • Respect ASL and Deaf culture and spread awareness about it throughout the school
  • Expand our ASL vocabulary to deepen our conversational skills

ASL 2 Period 3:

  • To apply our skills more by signing with each other outside of class
  • To be able to spread more awareness even outside of school
  • To improve our signing skills and our confidence to sign

ASL 1 Period 5:

  • To spread Deaf awareness to our school community
  • Have full conversations in ASL
  • Be kind, understanding, and patient with each other as we all learn

ASL 1 Period 6:

  • Be supportive of all classmates as we learn together
  • Be confident in using our ASL skills
  • Experience class from the Deaf perspective

ASL 1 Period 8:

  • Get everyone involved and make ASL more than just a class
  • Create a respectful and happy learning environment
  • Always keep growing our ASL skills
